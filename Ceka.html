What Happens at CEKA?

Evening Parties

CEKA hosts vibrant evening parties where members gather to celebrate with music, dance, and cultural performances.

Stay in Hotels

Participants are accommodated in top-rated hotels ensuring comfort and convenience throughout the event.

Tourist Visits

The event includes guided tours of iconic tourist locations, giving everyone a chance to explore and appreciate the area.

Exhibition & Sale

A marketplace for local artisans and businesses to showcase their products, with opportunities to purchase unique items.

Networking

CEKA provides a platform for members to network, collaborate, and build lasting professional and personal connections.
