News

The President of Ekang Bese Y'Amerka recently participated in the 2025 New Year celebrations at the Embassy of the Republic of Cameroon in Washington, D.C. This event brought together various dignitaries and community leaders to usher in the New Year.

The President of Ekang Bese Y'Amerka proudly participated in the 2025 New Year Wishes Ceremony at the Embassy of the Republic of Cameroon in Washington, D.C. This prestigious gathering brought together distinguished dignitaries, community leaders, and members of the Cameroonian diaspora to exchange best wishes and strengthen ties for the year ahead.
As an organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Ekang cultural heritage, Ekang Bese Y'Amerka took this opportunity to reaffirm its presence and commitment to cultural engagement within the broader Cameroonian and African communities in the U.S. This participation underscores our mission to uphold ancestral values, unity, and cross-cultural dialogue, ensuring that the Ekang identity thrives across generations and borders.
Ekang Bese Y'Amerka remains committed to strengthening cultural diplomacy and fostering community development through continued engagement at local and international levels.
