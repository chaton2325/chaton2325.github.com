Our Mission

Promoting and preserving Ekang culture in America

The EBYA executive board works tirelessly to organize cultural events, maintain traditions, and foster community among the Ekang diaspora in America.

Through initiatives like CEKA and Nda Ekang Y'Amerka, we create platforms for cultural expression, education, and celebration of our heritage.
