About EBYA

Empowering communities through action and unity

Our Story & Mission

EBYA was founded on the belief that communities thrive when they work together. We are a community-driven organization dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through education, support, and collective action.

Our mission is to create positive change by providing resources, opportunities, and support to those who need it most. We believe in the power of community and the importance of working together to build a better future for everyone.

Through our network of associations, partnerships, and community programs, we work tirelessly to preserve cultural heritage, foster education, and create lasting positive impact in communities across the United States and Canada.
