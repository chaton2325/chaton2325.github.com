CEKA 2026

Community Empowerment & Knowledge Alliance

The Most Anticipated Event of 2026

CEKA 2026 represents a groundbreaking opportunity for community leaders, activists, and change-makers to come together in New York City for an unprecedented gathering focused on empowerment, knowledge sharing, and collaborative action.

This conference will bring together over 1000 participants from across the United States, Canada, and beyond, representing diverse communities and organizations united by a common goal: creating lasting positive change in our societies.

Through keynote presentations, interactive workshops, networking sessions, and collaborative projects, CEKA 2026 will provide the tools, knowledge, and connections needed to amplify community impact and drive meaningful social transformation.
