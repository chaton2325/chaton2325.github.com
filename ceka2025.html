CEKA 2025

North American Ekang Federation

CEKA 2025 Festival

Join us from August 07th to 10th, 2025 in Chicago for a celebration of Ekang culture with performances, exhibitions, and more!

Event Highlights:

  • Date: August 07th - 10th, 2025
  • Location: Chicago, USA
  • Exhibitions: Ekang heritage, art, and craftsmanship
  • Live Performances: Bikutsi music, traditional dances, and more
  • Gala: Fundraising event for Ekang heritage preservation
