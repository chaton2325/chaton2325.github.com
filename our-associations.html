Our Associations

Partners in making a difference around the world

Building Stronger Communities Together

At Ebya, we believe that the strength of our community lies in the unity and collaboration of our diverse associations. Each organization brings unique perspectives, cultural heritage, and specialized expertise to create a powerful network of support and empowerment.

Our associations span across the United States and Canada, representing various Beti communities and cultural groups. From Chicago to Minnesota, from Atlanta to Columbus, these organizations work tirelessly to preserve traditions, foster community development, and create lasting positive change.

Through education, cultural preservation, social initiatives, and community outreach, our partner associations are making a real difference in the lives of thousands of people. They organize events, provide support services, facilitate cultural exchanges, and build bridges between communities.
